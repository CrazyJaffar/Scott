Standard 90 Day Warranty

Scott Auto Parts Parts are covered by a Standard 90 Day Warranty. Extended Warranty Options are available for Purchase. See Our Extended Warranty packages below. Please refer to our customer support professionals to determine what warranty upgrade package fits your specific needs.
